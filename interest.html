Interests

During my free time, I would have the enjoyment on playing my guitar. I like the sound of acoustic guitars and I love making fingerstyle guitar covers on YouTube where I play popular songs. I highly recommend checking out my YouTube channel.

One of my favorite hobbies is speedcubing. Speedcubing is a sport where you solve Rubik's cubes or twisty puzzles as fast as you can. I can solve a cube about around 10/9/8 seconds, and my best single time was in 5 seconds. The one thing that makes speedcubing fun is the competitions and the community. Without that, speedcubing wouldn't be that special.

The one sport that I'm doing since I was around 7 or 8 years old was Taekwondo. Without Taekwondo, I wouldn't be the person that I am today. I have learned so much knowledge from Taekwondo and it help me to be more confident. As of this time, I've been a black belt for about 4 years now and I'm currently a second-degree black belt. I haven't been to TKD classes for quite a while due to my busy work and school schedule.
